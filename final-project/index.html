How do I make them?

For each map, I start by selecting a region to focus on and the temples I want to feature. I then explore the general architecture and geography of each temple to understand how best to represent them in a unique way. The creation process begins in ArcGIS Pro, where I design a cartographic 3D map of the region. I then import a photo of this map as a layer into Adobe Illustrator.

Using this as a guide, I carefully illustrate the temples on top of the map layer, adding detailed touches to enhance the overall aesthetic. I have decided to also create maps that include the future sites of temples if their renderings are available. After some finishing touches of the map such as labels and styling, it leaves with the final result, a one-of-a-kind artistic map that combines accuracy with creativity, highlighting the beauty and uniqueness of each temple.
