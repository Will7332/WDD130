Home page

Welcome to my LDS Temple Map Portfolio! Here, you'll find a collection of digital, illustrationed 3D maps of LDS Temples in different areas. Each map is a unique blend of creativity and devotion, capturing the beauty and significance of these sacred places in a fun and artistic way. Whether you're a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints or simply appreciate the architecture and geography of these temples, I hope you enjoy exploring my work! For each map, I start by selecting a region to focus on and the temples I want to feature. I then explore the general architecture and geography of each temple to understand how best to represent them in a unique way. The creation process begins in ArcGIS Pro, where I design a cartographic 3D map of the region. I then import a photo of this map as a layer into Adobe Illustrator. Using this as a guide, I carefully illustrate the temples on top of the map layer, adding detailed touches to enhance the overall aesthetic. I have decided to also create maps that include the future sites of temples if their renderings are available. After some finishing touches of the map such as labels and styling, it leaves with the final result, a one-of-a-kind artistic map that combines accuracy with creativity, highlighting the beauty and uniqueness of each temple. Here is a list of the maps I have made so far. Go to my maps page to explore the maps I have made! IDAHO FLORIDA COLORADO NORTHEAST MIDWEST CALIFORNIA CENTRAL MEXICO Maps in progress: TEXAS CANADA

Explore Maps

Idaho Temples Map Here is my first cartographic temple map! As a student at BYU-Idaho, I have been inspired to illustrate the local temples and the geography of the state, starting out with the Rexburg temple, followed by the Idaho Falls temple. After completing the current temples in operation, I decided to include future temples for anyone interested. I used the renders released by the church to create these illustrations based off of them in their respective locations. Florida Temples Map My second cartographic temple map, I decided to do my home state of Florida. I have grown up going to the Orlando temple, the only temple in Florida for a long time. While it was only a half hour drive for me, seeing the growth of the church in my state the past decade leading to now 5 total temples being either announced or constructed has brought me joy, leading to the inspiration of creating this map. Colorado Temples Map Having served my mission in Colorado, I knew I had to make a temples map of this state. I served in the Colorado Springs mission, where the nearest temple was Denver. However The CO Springs temple was recently announced and will be the 4th temple to be built in the state. Northeast Temples Map Growing up I have taken many road trips along the East Coast, and have explored many places in the Northeastern part of the country. This map displays the unique array of temples in this region, particularly based off of the colonial roots of the US. Midwest Temples Map There have been significant church events that have occured within this region, in particular with Ohio and Illinois. I have also had the privelage of visiting church sites in this region, including in Nauvoo and western Missouri. California Temples Map I first created a map solely for temples in Southern California because there were enough to fit in one map. After creating that map I thought to myself I might as well the rest of California. The state has a total of 12 temples either dedicated, constructed or announced. Central Mexico Map I have personal family roots from this region, which led to the inspiration of creating the Mexico City temple where my parents got sealed, and the new Puebla temple which is where my mother's family is from.

About Me

Hello! I'm William Hanford, I am originally from Orlando, Florida and come from a smaller family with one sister. I served a full time LDS mission in Colorado, and after returning home I earned my Associate's Degree at my community college in Orlando. I transferred to Brigham Young University - Idaho where I am currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Graphic with a UI/UX emphasis. I also have a minor in Geographic Information Systems (GIS), since I have always had an interest in maps as well. Cartography is something that I have always found fascinating, which combines tools of Graphic Design and GIS. I decided to explore that by creating these maps of temples!
